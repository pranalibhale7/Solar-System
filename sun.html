The Sun

The Heart of Our System

The Sun is a massive, glowing sphere of hot gas at the center of our solar system. It provides the light, heat, and energy that makes life on Earth possible.

Every planet, asteroid, and comet in our solar system orbits the Sun. Its immense gravity holds everything together, while nuclear fusion in its core produces the energy that has sustained our system for 4.6 billion years.

Type Yellow Dwarf Star
Age 4.6 billion years
Temperature 5,500°C (Surface)
Contains 99.86% of solar system's mass
